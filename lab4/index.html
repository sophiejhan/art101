Challenge

The challenge of this lab was trying to get into the habit of thinking in terms of pseudocoding before we jump into coding.

Problems

We didn't run into any big problems this lab.

Results

The results of this lab are these lists:

This list describes the steps in making a vanilla latte:


				Making a vanilla latte:
				1. Pour milk into steaming pitcher.
				2. Steam the milk.
				3. Make desired amount of espresso.
				4. Pump in amount of vanilla syrup into mug for desired sweetness.
				5. Remove milk from steamer and wipe down steaming wand.
				6. Purge steaming wand.
				7. Groom the milk by tapping the pitcher on the table.
				8. Pour espresso shots into the mug.
				9. Pour milk into the mug.
